What this app does

WattsNear helps you find EV charging stations using publicly available data from the U.S. Department of Energy's NREL Alternative Fueling Station Locator. Station data is maintained by NREL and third-party charging networks — it may be inaccurate, out of date, or incomplete.

Trip range calculator

The trip range calculator is an estimate based on numbers you provide. Real-world EV range depends on temperature, speed, cargo, and terrain. Do not rely solely on the calculator for safety-critical driving decisions.

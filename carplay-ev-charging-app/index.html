Why WattsNear is the best CarPlay EV charging app

Live U.S. DOE data

Every charger is sourced from the U.S. Department of Energy's NREL database — the same data the government uses — updated daily. No stale listings from years ago.

Sorted by price, not just distance

Most EV apps sort by proximity. WattsNear sorts by price too, so you can decide whether the closest charger is worth the premium over the cheaper one two exits away.

Zero tracking, zero account

How it works in CarPlay

  1. Plug in your iPhone or connect wirelessly. WattsNear appears in the CarPlay app grid automatically.
  2. Tap WattsNear on your dashboard screen. The app loads chargers near your current GPS location in under two seconds.
  3. Pick a station by distance or price. Tap to get turn-by-turn directions in Maps — never leaving CarPlay.
